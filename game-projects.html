Fantasy RP Game

A true role-play experience built in Ureal Engine 5 with Blueprints and C++. This game encourages players to create their own stories and scenes by role-playing as their characters. Players will be able to own shops, craft various weapons and armor, own homes, adventure around the world, and more. The goal of this game is to give players an Isekai experience into another world!
