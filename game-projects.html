Fantasy Roleplay Game

A fully immersive role-playing experience developed in Unreal Engine 5. This game invites players to craft their own stories and live out unique adventures by stepping into the roles of their characters. Players can own and operate shops, craft weapons and armor, build and personalize homes, explore a vast open world, and much more. The ultimate goal is to deliver an authentic isekai experience, transporting players to a richly detailed fantasy world where their imagination sets the limits!

Technology Used:

  • Unreal Engine 5
  • Blueprints
  • C++
  • Brushify
  • Ultra Dynamic Sky
  • MetaHuman
  • Steam Online Subsystem
  • Git/GitHub

Urban Fantasy (SNHU Project)

While working toward my Game Programming and Development Bachelor's Degree, I worked in a team of 5, my role was the UIX and SFX developer. Teeth (SNHU Project)

This was a solo developed game as part of a class. The game was a looter/shooter style zombie survival game built in Unreal Engine 5 with Blueprints. The player was to survive a zombie hoard and make it to the boss, a dentist that was converting people into zombies! The player had to find a weapon and use limited amount of ammo to make it to the boss fight and defeat the "Dentist" who was the final boss.

Technology Used:

  • Unreal Engine 5
  • Blueprints
  • Git/GitHub
